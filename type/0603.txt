Global Theme Park Attendance Reaches 500 Million

Global attendance at the 10 biggest operators of theme parks increased 4% last year,
reaching 500 million for the first time, according to a recent report.

The report by the Themed Entertainment Association and Economics  Practice at
AECOM said global attendance at waterparks also increased by 2.5%, while attendance at
the top museums changed little compared to the year before.

The report said new attractions based on popular movies or books, such as the land
based on Toy Story at Disney's Hollywood Studios, brought many more poeple to visit
North American parks.

Attendance is expected to continue to grow this year with the opening of other
attractions based on movies
