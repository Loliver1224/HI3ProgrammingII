Millions Use 123456 as Online Password

Millions of people use easy-to-guess passwords for their online accounts, making it very
simple for criminals to hack them.

Data released by the UK's National Cyber Security Centre(NCSC) showed that 123456
was the most common password used on accounts that have been hacked, with 23
million hacked accounts around the world having used it.

The second most common password on hacked accounts was 123456789, while "qwerty"
and "password" were also in the top five.

A lot of accounts also had first names as a password, with the most common one
being Ashley. The next most common were Michael, Daniel, Jessica and Chaerlie.
