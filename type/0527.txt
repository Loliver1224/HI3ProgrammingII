Instagram Influence 50% of British Vacation Choices

More than 50% of British tourists choose where they go on vacation basaed on the photos
they see on Instagram, according to research done for travel conpany easyjet.

A survey of 2,000 British adults aged between 18 and 65 found that 55% have booked a
trip because they saw a destination on the social media website.

The survey also found thet 32% choose where they go on holiday because of how good
the photosthey tale there will look online, and 30% say they have shared vacation
photos to make their friends and family jealous.

The same research reports that people take an avarage of 2,500 photos during a one-
week vacation, as they try to find the perfect photo to post online.
