Japanese Kids Spending Longer Online

The most recent survey from Japan's Cabinet Office has found that young people in the
country are spending more time online than ever.

Over 93% of children aged 10 to 17 used the internet in 2018, going online for an average
of 2 hours 49 minutes every day - 9 minutes more than in 2017. They most often used
the internet to watch videos, play games, and talk to others.

Over a third of primary school kids, three quarters of middle school kids and 99% of high
school kids now own a smartphone in Japan.
