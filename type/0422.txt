Israeli Spacecraft Crashes in Attempt to Land on Moon

An Israeli Spacecraft Crashed into the moon on April 11 just moments
before it touched the ground, failing in an attempt to make history as
the first privately funded moon landing.

"We ... Crashes on the surface of the moon, " said Opher Doron of Israeli
Aerospace Industries, who built the Spacecraft along with SpacelL. He
said the Spacecraft's engine turned off shortly before landing, and
scientists were still trying to find out the cause.

Still, Doron called the mission an "amazing success," for reaching the
moon and coming so close to landing successfully.
